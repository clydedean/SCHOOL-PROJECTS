Discussion Board

Why is encryption important? (value and usage of encryption)

Answer: Encryption is crucial for protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access. It ensures confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity in digital communications and storage.

Can encryption be misused? (scenarios)

Answer: Yes, encryption can be misused in various scenarios such as hiding illegal activities or protecting malware.

Ethical concerns about encryption

Answer: There are ethical concerns about law enforcement access and privacy, such as the debate on “backdoors” in encryption systems.
